# List of Player Skills

This section lists all {term}`Skill`s that are available to 
{term}`Player Character`s.


(skills:list:core)=
## The Core Skills

All {term}`Character`s have access to all {term}`Core Skill`s and automatically
have a {term}`Level` of of 1 in each.

* [Fitness](skills:core:fitness)
  * [Strength](skills:core:strength)
  * [Endurance](skills:core:endurance)
  * [Agility](skills:core:agility)
* [Communication](skills:core:communication)
  * [Charisma](skills:core:charisma)
  * [Deception](skills:core:deception)
  * [Insight](skills:core:insight)
* [Acumen](skills:core:acumen)
  * [Instinct](skills:core:instinct)
  * [Observation](skills:core:observation)
  * [Memory](skills:core:memory)


(skills:list:mundane)=
## The Mundane Skills

All {term}`Humanoid` {term}`Character`s have access to all 
{term}`Mundane Skill`s and automatically have a {term}`Level` of 1 in each.

* [Combat](skills:mundane:combat)
  * [Kinetics](skills:mundane:kinetics)
  * [Tactics](skills:mundane:tactics)
  * [Armaments](skills:mundane:armaments)
* [Craft](skills:mundane:craft)
  * [Art](skills:mundane:arg)
  * [Engineering](skills:mundane:engineering)
  * [Survival](skills:mundane:survival)
  * [Stealth](skills:mundane:stealth)
* [Reason](skills:mundane:reason)
  * [Logic](skills:mundane:logic)
  * [Scholarship](skills:mundane:scholarship)
  * [Investigation](skills:mundane:investigation)


(skills:list:path)=
## The Path Skills

All {term}`PC`s and some {term}`NPC`s have access to the {term}`Path Skill`s.
{term}`Path Skill`s represent means of accessing the 
{term}`Exceptional Skill`s, which are typically each compatible with a subset
of {term}`Path Skill`s.




(skills:list:exceptional)=
## The Exceptional Skills

All {term}`PC`s and some {term}`NPC`s have access to the
{term}`Exceptional Skill`s. {term}`Exceptional Skill`s represent supernatural
abilities that are accessed via {term}`Path Skill`s.
