# Character Advancement

Over the course of a {term}`Campaign`, {term}`Player Character`s are expected
to grow and develop in knowledge, depth, and power. A substantial part of this
growth occurs through advancement of the {term}`Campaign`'s story, such as when
the {term}`PC`s discovery a secret or develop a relationship with an
{term}`NPC`. Additionally, the advancement of a {term}`PC`'s potential is
represented by the advancement of their {term}`Skill` {term}`Level`s.

Advancement can occur in a number of ways during a {term}`Campaign`. The most
common reasons that a {term}`Skill` advances is due to the following, all of
which are described in the sections below:
* Using the Learn and Teach {term}`Abilities<Ability>` of the
  [Reason](skills:mundane:reason) and [Scholarship](skills:mundane:scholarship)
  {term}`Skill`s.
* Using the Study {term}`Ability` of the
  [Investigation](skills:mundane:investigation) {term}`Skill`, the Train
  {term}`Ability` of the [Tactics](skills:mundane:tactics) {term}`Skill`, or
  the Practice {term}`Ability` of the [Engineering](skills:mundane:engineering)
  {term}`Skill`.
* Spending an {term}`Exceptional Point` to advance a {term}`Path Skill` or an
  {term}`Domain Skill` during {term}`Ellipsis`.
* An award by the {term}`Narrator` due to events of the story.
