# The Characters of Tu'urna

The cast of {term}`Character`s of Tu'urna encompasses the spectrum of creatures
(and sometimes entities that are merely creature-like) that one can encounter
in the game world.

{term}`Character`s fall primarily into two categories:
{term}`Player Character`s and {term}`Nonplayer Character`s, the latter of which
can be further subcategorized.
